An unforgettable night in Skadarlija

In the Bohemian Heart of Belgrade

Welcome to the street that never sleeps. The street they sang about songs and stories, the refuge of great painters, writers and poets. A street where tradition is nurtured, a landmark and a symbol of the city of Belgrade.

Experience the spirit of old Belgrade with old town songs and traditional food. Bohemian evening in Skadarlija is filled with song, story, delicious food and the best wine.

Something For Everyone

The restaurant's offer is based on a combination of traditional and modern dishes where every guest can find something for themselves for breakfast, lunch or dinner. We recommend to our guests sardines in greens, top steaks and sweet dishes, of which the bohemian cake and schwarzwald schnita stand out, which will give your taste buds the ultimate pleasure. All meals are accompanied by an offer several domestic wines, pressed juices as well as other hot drinks drinks.
